Professional Summary

สรุปประสบการณ์

ສະຫຼຸບປະສົບການ

Experienced IT Specialist with over 10 years in database development, system administration, and technical training. Proven track record in implementing IT solutions across 18 provinces in Laos, with expertise in safety management and digital transformation.
